In Slack Calculator

Need to do a calculation while doing your work on Slack? Now, no need to leave slack at all!

Usage

Type /calc followed by any expression, normal or scientific, to evaluate.

  • /calc help
  • /calc 2 + 2
  • /calc (2.25 * 76.0) * 18
  • /calc 8 ^ 2
  • /calc sqrt(2)
  • /calc sin(2.0)
  • /calc log(2.0)
  • /calc exp(2.0)
  • /calc max(sin(2.0), tan(2.0))
  • /calc min(exp(2.0), log(2.0))
